# Video Brief: The AI-Enabled History Teacher

**Project:** 30-second video for Tommy — aspiring history teacher
**Brand:** Tomorrow's Classroom
**Primary Color:** #1B3A6B (navy)
**Accent Color:** #C9A84C (gold)
**Duration:** 30 seconds

---

## Brief (paste into Video Magic wizard)

```
VIDEO BRIEF: The AI-Enabled History Teacher

BRAND NAME: Tomorrow's Classroom
PRIMARY COLOR: #1B3A6B
ACCENT COLOR: #C9A84C

BRAND CHARACTER: Young male history teacher in his mid-twenties,
professional but approachable, wearing a neat button-down shirt,
confident and passionate expression, clean modern style.

VIDEO DURATION: 30 seconds
TARGET AUDIENCE: Aspiring history teachers entering the profession

---

SCENE 1 — The Call (0–7s)
Visual: A young passionate teacher commands the front of a modern
classroom. Students lean forward, captivated. Warm light. Energy
and enthusiasm fill the room. Dynamic camera movement.

Voiceover: "Tommy, the greatest teachers in history didn't just
teach facts — they changed how people see the world. You're about
to become one of them."

---

SCENE 2 — The Transformation (7–15s)
Visual: A futuristic classroom where history comes alive. Students
are visibly transported — Revolutionary soldiers march across a
glowing display, World War II aerial maps illuminate, a divided
nation pulses with tension. Awe and wonder on every face.

Voiceover: "AI is transforming every classroom — letting students
walk the streets of Revolutionary Philadelphia, witness the battles
of World War II, feel the tension of a nation dividing."

---

SCENE 3 — The Human Gift (15–22s)
Visual: Teacher in close one-on-one conversation with a student.
A historical artifact rests between them. Warm intimate light.
A moment of genuine human connection that no technology can replace.

Voiceover: "But no algorithm can do what you do. No AI can make
a student feel why the past matters — or why it's still alive
today. That's yours alone."

---

SCENE 4 — The Future (22–30s)
Visual: Wide sweeping shot of a vibrant, fully engaged classroom.
Students energized and curious. The teacher stands at the front
with quiet confidence, in command of both the room and the tools
of tomorrow.

Voiceover: "The AI-enabled history teacher doesn't have less to
do — they have more power to inspire. Your students are waiting.
Your classroom is ready."

---

CALL TO ACTION: Embrace the tools. Keep the passion.
```
